## Soccer Squares Pool

### What is Soccer Squares?
A squares pool is a grid-based betting game. Players buy squares on a grid; at halftime and full time, score final digits determine winners.

### Grid Sizes
| Size | Squares | Use Case |
|------|---------|----------|
| 10x10 | 100 | Large pools |
| 5x5 | 25 | Small groups |
| 6x6 | 36 | Medium groups |

### How It Works
1. Create grid with home team on one axis, away team on other
2. Each square = pair of score digits (e.g., 3-1)
3. Players buy squares at fixed price
4. After all sold, randomly assign digits 0-9 to each axis
5. Payouts at halftime and full time

### Payout Structure Example
- Price per square: $2
- Total: $200
- Halftime prize: 40% = $80
- Full-time prize: 60% = $120

## Other Popular Soccer Betting Methods

### Score Predictions
- Predict exact score or goal difference
- Points awarded for accuracy

### Goal-Count Bets (Over/Under)
- Wager on total goals in match

### Prop Bets
- Who scores first
- Number of corners
- Yellow cards

### Season-Long Fantasy/Accumulators
- Predict outcomes over weekend/week

### "Kit" Pools
- Team trivia or in-game events

## Best Practices

### Transparency
- Publish grid, ownership, payout rules BEFORE selling

### Documentation
- Track: Square #, Player Name, Price, Assigned digits

### Communication
- Use single channel (group chat, email, shared doc)

### Etiquette
- Small, proportional prizes
- Ensure legal age compliance

## Setup Checklist

- [ ] Choose grid size (10x10, 5x5, etc.)
- [ ] Set price per square
- [ ] Define payout structure
- [ ] Sell squares and track ownership
- [ ] Randomize digits AFTER all squares sold
- [ ] Share grid with players
- [ ] Determine winners at halftime/final
- [ ] Distribute prizes
