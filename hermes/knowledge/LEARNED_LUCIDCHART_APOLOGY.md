## Apology Content & Visuals

### 5-Step Framework

#### Step 1: Learn Communication Framework
- Crisis communication guidelines
- Personal accountability models
- Nonviolent Communication (NVC)
- Leadership accountability

#### Step 2: Practice Crafting Apologies
Templates for different scenarios:
- Public apology
- Internal apology
- Customer service apology
- Leadership accountability

**Template Elements:**
- Acknowledge impact
- Take responsibility
- Offer remedy
- Outline corrective action

#### Step 3: Visualize the Process
- Diagram tool to map apology workflow
- **Process Flow Elements:**
  - What happened
  - Who's affected
  - What went wrong
  - Actions taken
  - Timeline
  - Next steps
- Decision points (e.g., "Is additional action required?")

#### Step 4: Review and Refine
- Get peer/mentor feedback
- Test tone, clarity, credibility
- A/B test different phrasings
- Use low-stakes contexts (internal memos, mock messages)

#### Step 5: Build Reusable Apology Kit
- **Templates:**
  - Email template
  - Social post template
  - Internal memo template
- **Process Diagram** (one-page)
- **Checklist for Credibility Cues:**
  - Timeliness
  - Specificity
  - Accountability
  - Remedies

### Best Practices
- Respond quickly (timeliness matters)
- Be specific about what happened
- Take clear responsibility
- Provide concrete remedies
- Outline preventive actions
- Follow up
