## 9-Step Implementation Process

1. Define guiding principles and scope
   - Zero-based vs baseline budgeting
   - Consolidation needs

2. Map data lineage
   - Data sources (ERP GL, HR, sales)
   - Data owners

3. Choose approach and tool
   - Scalability
   - Multi-entity support
   - Integration ease

4. Model core budgets
   - Revenue, COGS, OpEx, capex, cash flow
   - Drivers and assumptions

5. Establish governance
   - Roles, approvals, versioning
   - Audit trails

6. Align with accounting
   - GL structure
   - Accounting periods
   - Closing calendars

7. Implement reporting
   - BVA dashboards
   - Management reports
   - Scenario comparison

8. Train and onboard
   - Role-based training

9. Iterate and improve
   - Periodic model accuracy reviews
