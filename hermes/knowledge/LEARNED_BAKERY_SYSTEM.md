## Pricing & Costing System

### Naming Convention

#### Cakes
Format: CAKE-[size]-[servings]-[design]
- CAKE-6in-20-basic
- CAKE-8in-40-ornate
- CAKE-2Tier-60-wedding
- Add DEPOSIT tag: CAKE-8in-40-ornate-DEPO

#### Cookies
Format: COOK-[dozen/box]-[quantity]-[style]
- COOK-DOZEN-24-classic
- COOK-BOX-50-holiday
- COOK-DOZEN-12-gourmet

---

### Cost Categories

1. **Ingredient costs** - flour, sugar, butter, eggs, fillings, chocolate, decorations, toppers
2. **Packaging** - boxes, ribbons, padding
3. **Labor** - mixing, baking, cooling, decorating, packaging
4. **Overhead** - utilities, kitchen wear, cleaning supplies (pro-rated)
5. **Misc** - seeds, edible images, special molds

---

### Costing Formula

**Per item cost** = (Ingredient + Packaging + Labor + Overhead)

**Price** = Cost × (1 + Margin)

**Net Profit** = Price − Cost

---

### Examples

#### Cake 6-inch (serves 20)
- Ingredients: $6
- Packaging: $1
- Labor: 2 hours × $18/hr = $36
- Overhead: $2
- **Cost**: $45
- Margin: 40%
- **Price**: $45 × 1.40 = $63
- **Profit**: $18

#### Cookie Box (DOZEN-24 classic)
- Ingredients: $5
- Packaging: $1
- Labor: 1 hour × $18 = $18
- Overhead: $1
- **Cost**: $25
- Margin: 50%
- **Price**: $25 × 1.50 = $37.50
- **Profit**: $12.50

## Flat-Rate Pricing Model

### Flat Rate Options
- **Single flat price** per item (e.g., 6" cake = $X)
- **Tiered flat rates** (small, medium, large)
- Add-ons as separate line items

---

### Cost Inputs (Even with Flat Pricing)

Need to track:
- Flat price charged (P)
- Direct ingredient cost (C)
- Packaging cost (F)
- Labor cost (L) = hours × hourly rate
- Overhead allocation (O)
- Delivery/other costs (D)

---

### Formulas

**Gross Profit** = P − (C + F + L + O + D)

**Net Profit** = Gross Profit − (payment fees, delivery, marketing)

**Simple Pricing**: P = (C + F + L + O) × (1 + Gross Margin %)

---

### Quick Template (Spreadsheet)

| Item | Flat Price (P) | Ingredients (C) | Packaging (F) | Labor (L) | Overhead (O) | Delivery (D) | Gross Profit | Net Profit |
|------|---------------|------------------|---------------|-----------|---------------|---------------|--------------|------------|
| 6" Cake | 63 | 6 | 1 | 36 | 2 | 0 | 18 | 18 |

**Formula:**
- Gross Profit: =P - (C + F + L + O + D)

---

### Practical Tips

1. **Be honest** - Track actual orders to refine estimates
2. **Use a buffer** - Increase price slightly if unsure
3. **Delivery** - Include in price or as separate add-on
4. **Simple for clients** - Clean price list, calculator behind scenes
