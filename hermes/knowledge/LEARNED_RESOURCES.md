## Chart & Visualization Best Practices

### Key Principles (from storytellingwithdata.com)
- Don't just show data - tell a story with it
- Make graphs that make sense
- Present compelling, action-inspiring stories
- Customize with examples from your organization
- Communicate data confidently

### Color Palette Resources

#### ColorBrewer (colorbrewer2.org)
- **Sequential**: For ordered data (low → high)
- **Diverging**: For data with a critical midpoint
- **Qualitative**: For categorical data (no order)
- **Colorblind safe**: Always check accessibility
- **Print friendly**: Consider black & white printing

#### Recommended Palettes for Dashboards
- **Sequential**: Blues, greens, purples (light to dark)
- **Diverging**: Red-White-Blue or custom center
- **Qualitative**: Distinct, non-clashing colors

### Accessibility (WCAG)
- High contrast ratios for text
- Colorblind-safe palettes
- Don't rely on color alone for meaning
- Add labels, patterns, or text

## Modern Dashboard Design Trends

### Visual Style
- Gradient fills (not flat colors)
- Glassmorphism (frosted glass effect)
- Rounded corners (2xl, full)
- Card-based layouts
- White space / breathing room
- Subtle shadows
- Micro-animations

### Chart Types
- **Bar charts**: Comparisons
- **Line charts**: Trends over time
- **Donut charts**: Parts of a whole
- **Pie charts**: Avoid if possible (hard to read)
- **Progress bars**: Goal tracking
- **Sparklines**: Compact trends

### Layout Principles
- Most important info at top
- Group related items
- Consistent spacing
- Clear hierarchy
- Mobile-responsive

## Power BI Visualization Types

### Charts for Comparison & Trends
- **Bar/Column charts** - Compare values across categories
- **Line charts** - Track trends over time
- **Area charts** - Show cumulative totals
- **Combo charts** - Combine column + line
- **Ribbon charts** - Show rank changes over time
- **Waterfall charts** - Running total with +/-

### Charts for Part-to-Whole
- **Pie/Donut charts** - Parts of a whole (use sparingly)
- **Treemaps** - Hierarchical data as nested rectangles
- **Funnel charts** - Sequential processes (sales pipelines)

### Charts for Distribution & Relationships
- **Scatter charts** - Correlations between values
- **Bubble charts** - Scatter with size as third dimension

## Work Clothes & PPE (OSHA)

### Personal Protective Equipment (osha.gov)
- **PPE includes**: gloves, safety glasses, earplugs, hard hats, respirators, coveralls, vests
- **Requirements**:
  - Proper fit (comfortable, encourages use)
  - Employer must train workers on:
    - When PPE is needed
    - What kind is needed
    - How to properly use/maintain
    - Limitations, care, disposal
- **PPE Program** must address:
  - Hazards present
  - Selection & maintenance
  - Training
  - Monitoring effectiveness

### Key Principles
- All PPE should be safely designed & constructed
- Maintained in clean & reliable fashion
- Must meet ANSI standards
