# Business Rules — Marcelo's Decision Framework

> Core principles for evaluating opportunities, making decisions, and allocating time.
> Hermes-native — adapted from OpenClaw
> Last updated: 2026-05-07

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Cash Flow Over Speculation
- Always prefer $1,000/month repeatable over $50,000 one-time
- Real estate = monthly checks, not appreciation lottery
- Crypto bot = rules-based, not vibes
- If it can't generate monthly income, be skeptical

### 2. Protect Marcelo's Time
- Automation before hiring humans
- Bots handle the work; Marcelo makes decisions
- Never do manually what can be automated
- Time is the scarcest resource — spend it on high-value work only

### 3. Validate Before Scaling
- Test small before going big
- One property before five
- One client before 10
- One video before launching full channel
- If small doesn't work, big won't either

### 4. Rules-Based, Not Emotional
- Binance bot: EMA + RSI rules, not "feels like it's going up"
- Real estate: math first, emotion second
- Business decisions: data + logic + gut, in that order
- No FOMO-driven moves

### 5. Low-Hanging Fruit First
- $500/month x 3 beats $5,000/month x 0
- Solve real problems for real clients
- Use existing skills and network before learning new ones
- Marcelo's IT network is warm — use it

## Decision Rules by Category

### Crypto / Trading
- Bot follows strict rules — no manual overrides
- RSI > 80 = no buy (ever)
- 3% max risk per trade
- 6% daily loss limit = pause trading
- MIN_RR 2:1 before entry
- No new capital added until Cycle 1 target hit
- **See also:** `trading-rules.md` in this Space

### Real Estate
- Must work on paper before buying (rent > mortgage + taxes + insurance + management + maintenance)
- Property manager must be identified BEFORE close
- Airbnb must be legal in municipality
- US citizen can visit without visa issues
- USD accepted or stable currency
- No hurricane/flood zone properties

### Business / Clients
- Minimum $150/hr for consulting (VP-level work)
- No NDAs that limit future options
- No non-competes that restrict Marcelo's skills
- Payment upfront for new clients (or 50% deposit)
- Fixed-price projects require 50% margin minimum

### Content / YouTube
- Marcelo reviews before anything goes live
- Hermes generates voice (if ElevenLabs configured), drafts content
- No controversial political content
- No financial advice — educational only
- Affiliate links disclosed

### Time Allocation
- Day job: VP of IT (existing income)
- Side projects: automation business, real estate research, YouTube
- **Rule:** No side project that requires >10 hrs/week of Marcelo's time unless it's generating >$5K/month

## What NOT to Do

### Crypto
- ❌ Manual trading to "catch up" after a loss
- ❌ Ignoring the bot's stop-loss
- ❌ Adding more capital when emotionally down
- ❌ Chasing coins outside the approved pair universe

### Real Estate
- ❌ Buy without property manager confirmed
- ❌ Buy in markets with legal uncertainty
- ❌ Over-leverage (max 70% LTV)
- ❌ Complex legal structures before understanding basics

### Business
- ❌ Accept projects below minimum rate
- ❌ Sign long-term contracts before working with client
- ❌ Build custom solutions for one-off problems
- ❌ Compete on price instead of value

### Content
- ❌ Post without Marcelo's approval
- ❌ Click-bait titles that misrepresent content
- ❌ Respond to comments that bait controversy
- ❌ Copy competitors' content
