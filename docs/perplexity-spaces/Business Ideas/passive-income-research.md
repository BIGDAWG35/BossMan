## Passive Income Categories

### 1. Rental Real Estate
- **Target:** $500–$2,000/month per property after expenses
- **Markets:** Durango, Ixtapa, DR, Costa Rica, Jacksonville FL
- **Strategy:** Buy low, rent high via Airbnb/VRBO
- **Risk:** Low (if cash-flow positive from day 1)

### 2. Digital Products / SaaS
- **Target:** $500–$5,000/month per product
- **Examples:** Dashboards, automation tools, content engines
- **Strategy:** Build once, sell many times
- **Risk:** Medium (market fit uncertain)

### 3. Content Monetization
- **YouTube:** Crypto/AI content, affiliate links, ad revenue
- **Strategy:** Consistent posting, affiliate partnerships
- **Risk:** Low (time investment, slow to scale)

### 4. Automation Consulting
- **Target:** $5,000–$25,000/month at $150–$300/hour
- **Focus:** Schools, law firms, small businesses with broken processes
- **Strategy:** Network + results → referrals
- **Risk:** Low (leveraging existing skills)
